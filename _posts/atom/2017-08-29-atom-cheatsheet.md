### Symbols view

- `^ ⌥ down` - jump to declaration under cursor
- `^ ⇧ r` - show tags

### Bracket matcher

- `^ m` - go to matching bracket
- `^ ]` - remove brackets from selection
- `^ ⌘ m` - select inside brackets
- `⌥ ⌘ .` - close tag
